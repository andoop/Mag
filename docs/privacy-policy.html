Privacy Policy for Mag

Effective date: 2026-05-22
Last updated: 2026-05-22

This Privacy Policy describes how Mag handles information.

Mag is provided by an individual developer, not by a large platform or advertising network.

Contact email for privacy questions is provided below.

1. Summary

  • Mag does not require you to create an account with the developer.
  • Mag does not sell your personal information.
  • Mag does not include in-app advertising.
  • Most content you create in Mag is stored locally on your device.
  • If you choose to connect Mag to a third-party AI service, model API, OAuth service, or other external tool, the data required for that request will be sent to that service at your direction.

2. Information Stored by the App

Mag may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Chat history, session history, and tool execution results.
  • Workspace information and files you choose to open or preview.
  • App preferences and local settings.
  • Provider settings, API configuration, and other values you choose to save.
  • Secrets that you explicitly save, such as API keys or secret variables, which may be stored using device secure storage where supported.

3. What the Developer Collects

The developer does not intentionally collect your chat content, files, or personal information on the developer's own servers by default.

If a future version of Mag enables analytics or another hosted service, this Privacy Policy will be updated before that change is relied on for public distribution.

4. When Data May Leave Your Device

Data may be transmitted outside your device only when you choose to use a feature that depends on an external service. Examples include:

  • Calling a third-party AI or model provider that you configure.
  • Using OAuth or web-based authorization pages.
  • Connecting to an MCP server or another external tool endpoint.
  • Opening third-party web content inside the app.
  • Using the system share sheet to share content outside the app.

In those cases, the privacy practices of the relevant third party will apply to the data sent to that service.

5. Permissions

Mag requests device permissions only for features you actively use.

Permission Purpose
Camera To let you capture photos when you choose to do so.
Microphone / Audio Recording To let you record audio or use voice input features.
Notifications To show app status notifications when relevant features are in use.
Display over other apps To support the optional floating window feature.
Internet access To load online content or connect to third-party services you use.

6. Data Sharing

The developer does not sell your personal information and does not share your information with data brokers.

Data is shared only in these limited cases:

  • When you explicitly use a third-party service through the app.
  • When sharing is required by applicable law.
  • When sharing is reasonably necessary to protect security, rights, or prevent abuse.

7. Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete it in the app, clear the app's local storage, or uninstall the app.

Data sent to third-party services is governed by those third parties' retention practices.

8. Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect locally stored information. However, no software, device, or network transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Children's Privacy

Mag is not intended for children. If you believe personal information of a child has been provided through the app, please contact the developer.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be posted at this page with an updated effective date.
